Meat Balls Recipe

its the dish that resembles the testicles of a human and tastes like shit and urine

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Seasoning and herbs
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Italian Bread
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  1. Combine the first eight ingredients in a bowl
  2. Add the bread crumbs and slowly add the water
  3. Shape the mixture into meat balls
  4. Fry the meatballs until they are brown and crisp
